Above It All

Salesforce Tower is the first-of-its-kind workspace designed specifically to promote the health and well-being of its inhabitants. LEED Core and Shell Certified Platinum. Innovative outside air intakes on every floor provide outdoor-fresh air to each occupant to support health and wellness.

Over 13-foot high ceilings and 10-foot high continuous clear glass provide abundant daylight and a sense of openness and freedom.

The Blackwater recycling system is the largest onsite water recycling system in a commercial high-rise building in the United States, and the first of its kind in San Francisco. The Blackwater system reduces the potable water usage by 7.8 million gallons a year.

Salesforce Tower uses 50% less energy per square foot than the median United States office building and sources 100% renewable electricity.
